I. Brand Strategy

Foundation. Every decision flows from here.

Mission

Democratize business intelligence for Russian SMBs.

We believe every business owner deserves the same depth of analysis that McKinsey delivers to corporations — at a price that makes sense and a speed that matters. BSR turns 90 seconds and 10K roubles into a 360-degree audit that would otherwise cost 500K+ and weeks of waiting.

Vision

To become the default answer to the question «What's really going on with this business?» in Russia and CIS by 2028.

Brand Promise

«Full picture. No blind spots. 90 seconds.»

We promise to deliver a complete, verified, multi-angle business audit that a human analyst couldn't produce in under 2 weeks — in the time it takes to make coffee.

Brand Values

1. Transparency

Every number has a source. Every estimate is labelled. We show our work — calc-traces, confidence badges, data hierarchies. We don't hide behind «AI magic.»

2. Speed without shortcuts

Fast does not mean sloppy. 90 seconds of generation time backed by 40+ fact-checks, cross-verification across 2-3 sources per data point, and a dependency graph that cascades corrections.

3. Completeness

360 means 360. Finance + HR + Digital + Competitors + Strategy + Market. Not «here's a table of revenue» like SPARK. Not «here's a vague ChatGPT summary.» The whole picture.

4. Intellectual Honesty

If we don't know — we say so. Confidence badges (FACT / CALC / ESTIMATE) are core to the product. We'd rather show uncertainty than fake precision.

5. Design as Respect

Beautiful reports aren't vanity. They're respect for the reader's time and intelligence. Dark theme, gold accents, inline SVG — every detail is intentional.

Brand Personality — Archetype

The Sage + The Magician

Primary archetype: Sage — knowledge, truth, analysis, expertise. We are the advisor who sees what others miss.

Secondary archetype: Magician — transformation. We turn raw data into strategic insight in 90 seconds. The «how is this possible» moment.

Personality Traits

  • Confident, not arrogant
  • Precise, not pedantic
  • Direct, not rude
  • Sophisticated, not pretentious
  • Technical, but always accessible

Winner: «Полная картина. 90 секунд.»

Elevator Pitch

30 seconds

BSR — это AI-платформа, которая за 90 секунд генерирует полный аудит любого бизнеса в России: финансы, конкуренты, HR, digital, стратегия. То, за что консалтинг берёт 500 тысяч и две недели — мы делаем за 10 тысяч рублей и полторы минуты. Данные из ФНС, ЕГРЮЛ, маркетплейсов, соцсетей — верифицированные и прозрачные.

60 seconds

Представьте: вы вводите ИНН компании и через 90 секунд получаете HTML-отчёт на 25+ секций. Финансы из ФНС, конкурентный анализ на 8-12 компаний, SWOT, перцептуальная карта, HR-аудит с зарплатами из HH, digital-присутствие, цитаты лидеров отрасли и конкретную стратегию роста. Каждая цифра помечена: факт, расчёт или оценка. Каждый расчёт — с формулой и источниками.

СПАРК и Контур дают данные. ChatGPT даёт галлюцинации. Консалтинг даёт результат, но за 500K+ и 2-4 недели. BSR — единственный продукт на российском рынке, который даёт полный AI-аудит 360 с верификацией. Мы занимаем пустое пространство между бесплатным и дорогим.

Color Usage Rules

Correct

Gold ONLY for: brand elements, headings, interactive highlights, premium badges

Green = confirmed facts, positive metrics, growth

Blue = calculated values, info blocks, links

Orange = estimates, warnings, caution

Red = errors, negative metrics, decline

Wrong

Never use gold for body text

Never use red for non-negative information

Never use more than 2 semantic colors in one card

Never use pure white (#FFF) — always --text (#E8E4ED)

Never use pure black (#000) — always --bg (#0D0B0E)

IX. Positioning

Where BSR sits in the market and how we talk about competitors.

Positioning Statement

For owners of Russian SMBs (5-100M rub. revenue), investors, and business angels
Who need to understand a business quickly and completely before making decisions,
BSR is an AI-powered business audit platform
That generates a verified 360-degree report (finance + HR + digital + competitors + strategy) in 90 seconds,
Unlike SPARK/Kontur (data without analysis), ChatGPT (analysis without data), or consulting firms (analysis with data, but 500K+ and weeks),
BSR is the only product that combines completeness, speed, transparency, and affordability.

Competitive Messaging Framework

BSR vs SPARK / Kontur.Focus
They say: «We have all the data.»
We say: «Data is not insight. SPARK gives you 200 pages of tables. BSR gives you 25 sections of analysis with strategy, SWOT, and specific recommendations. Same data sources + AI that actually thinks.»
Key message: Data without analysis = noise. BSR = signal.
BSR vs Consulting (McKinsey, BCG, local firms)
They say: «You need human experts.»
We say: «You need speed + transparency. BSR shows every formula, every source, every assumption. A consultant gives you a PDF where the “how” is hidden behind a day rate. And it takes 2-4 weeks at 500K+. We take 90 seconds at 10K.»
Key message: Same depth, 1000x faster, 50x cheaper, fully transparent.
BSR vs ChatGPT / Free AI
They say: «Just ask GPT, it's free.»
We say: «ChatGPT hallucinates financial data. It doesn't check FNS, doesn't cross-verify, doesn't label confidence levels. BSR pulls real data from 10+ sources, verifies every fact, and shows you exactly what's a fact vs. estimate vs. calculation.»
Key message: Free AI = fake confidence. BSR = honest intelligence.
BSR vs AI.Insight / Other AI Analytics
They say: «We also use AI.»
We say: «AI is the tool, not the product. The product is the methodology: 11-stage pipeline, cross-verification protocol, dependency graph, calc-traces, 25+ sections. Show me another Russian product that does a full 360-audit with transparent calculations.»
Key message: AI is commodity. Methodology is the moat.

Key Messages by Segment

SMB Owners

«Understand your business like a corporate board does — without the corporate budget.»

Pain: No time/money for deep analysis. Deciding by gut feel.

Hook: 90 seconds. 10K rub. Full picture.

Proof: Show sample report with their industry.

«Due diligence that used to take 2 weeks. Now: before the pitch meeting ends.»

XI. Brand Rules Summary

The non-negotiables. Print this. Tape it to the wall.

Identity
  1. Dark theme only. No light mode (yet).
  2. Gold is brand. Green/Blue/Orange/Red are semantic. Never swap.
  3. Inter + JetBrains Mono. No other fonts.
  4. Logo: use provided SVGs. Never recreate.
  5. One HTML file, zero external dependencies, always.
Voice
  1. Russian language in all user-facing content.
  2. No hype words: «revolutionary», «unique», «best», «innovative».
  3. Every claim backed by data or marked as estimate.
  4. Educate first, sell second.
  5. Admit limitations openly.
Data Integrity
  1. Every number gets a confidence badge (FACT/CALC/ESTIMATE).
  2. Every calculation gets a calc-trace block.
  3. Source hierarchy: A (registries) > B (media) > C (industry) > D (aggregators) > E (self-report).
  4. Minimum 2 independent sources per fact.
  5. If only 1 source — mark it.
Product
  1. Reports are self-contained HTML. Print-ready.
  2. Inline SVG for all visualizations. No chart libraries.
  3. Mobile-responsive. Every table scrollable.
  4. BSR data point is ALWAYS gold in charts.
  5. Glossary section mandatory in every report.
